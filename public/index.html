Local Authority Map of Unemployment

An interactive map of local authorities in the UK. Click on a local authority to view information. The information is taken from unemployment data from the 2011 census which is hosted on the website ons.gov.uk. Unavailable data is shown in teal. This is due to changes in local authority structure between 2011 and 2015.
